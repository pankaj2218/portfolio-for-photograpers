Pankaj Sharma

Hi, it’s nice to meet you! My name is Pankaj Sharma. I’m a fine-art photographer based in Brooklyn, and I’ve been photographing for almost 8 years. I specialize in making portraits of strangers on the street both digitally and with film. Truth is stranger than fiction, and I’ve always been compelled by that fact, especially here in New York City. I’m represented by ClampArt, and I exhibit internationally. I’ve been published in The New York Times and my first monograph, Shoot the Arrow: A Portrait of The World Famous *BOB*, was published by Un-Gyve Press in 2013.
